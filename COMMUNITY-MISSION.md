# Community Mission

Compass exists to help people bring good ideas to life.

Many people carry useful, generous, creative, or practical ideas that never get built because daily life is heavy: time is scarce, energy is uneven, attention is fragmented, and the distance between intention and action can be brutal. This project is for reducing that distance.

The goal is not to build a product that extracts as much money, attention, or dependency as possible. The goal is to enable people: to help them think clearly, move steadily, make safer decisions, and turn more of their own values into real-world action.

That is why Compass and Latch are intended to be community-based and not-for-profit in spirit. The shared service should grow through trust, transparency, contribution, and mutual benefit. If it succeeds, people should be able to share what they have made, solved, learned, repaired, supported, or created with help from their companions.

The dream is a community where people lend each other capability without giving up autonomy: shared compute, shared patterns, shared safety work, shared stories, and shared momentum.

Every companion starts from the [Companion Anchor](./COMPANION-ANCHOR.md). Users can personalize their companion, but the shared anchor keeps the service pointed toward human flourishing, autonomy, honesty, safety, privacy, and constructive long-term growth.
